Job Description


• Regulatory Pharmacist •

Fleet Wood Pharmacy, located at Don Alfaro St., Tetuan, Z.C

Duties and Responsibilities:

• Able to secure quality documents/samples for Initial Registration, Amendments, Variations, Inclusions, Exhaustion, Shelf life Extension, PCPR, CLIDP's and the like.

• Able to submit compliance on or before the deadline prescribed by the FDA correctly and in a time efficient manner.

• Responsible to liaise necessary applications and to follow up all applications to FDA.

• Responsible for securing Licenses and or Renewals of the same (ie FDA Drugs, Cosmetics, Foods, DOH Accreditation's, DOH-Medical Devices, PDEA)

• Ensures that products being placed by the Import Department has the CPR prior to the arrival.

• Able to communicate with the suppliers and guide them accordingly on the Regulatory Requirements on the Registration of PDEA, BAI, and DOH Products.

• Secure quality documentation prior to application of Initial registration (FDA and DOH)

Qualifications and Skills:

• Candidate must possess at least a Bachelor's/College Degree, Professional License (Passed Board/Bar/Professional License Exam), Pharmacy/Pharmacology or equivalent.

• At least 1 year (s) of working experience in the related field is required in this position.

• Applicant must be willing to work in Makati City.

• Preferably 1-4 years Experienced Employees specializing in Healthcare- Pharmacy or equivalent.

• Full- time position (s) available.

• REGULATORY EXPERIENCE IS A MUST.

• Licensed Pharmacist.

• Can work under Minimum supervision.
